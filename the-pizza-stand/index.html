The pizza stand

You came to a fair when you see a pizza stand! Your family looks at the menu:

Menu

  • Cheese Pizza $5.99
  • Pepperoni Pizza $6.99
  • Pinapple Pizza $6.99
  • Everthing Pizza $14.99
  • Cookie Pizza $7.99
  • M&M Cookie Pizza $9.99
  • Sweet Delight Cookie Pizza $12.99

Mom says, "We shall eat pepperoni pizza. Pepperoni pizza tastes better than cheese pizza."

Dad agrees. Dad says, "May I have pepperoni pizza?"

The cashier accepts the order. Oh no. You don't like pepperoni pizza. What should you do?

Never mind. You can always take off the pepperoni. | Say to the cashier, "May I also have a cheese pizza?"
